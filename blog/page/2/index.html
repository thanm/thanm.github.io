Day 48: Lander WY to Dubois WY

Jun 6, 2025

Long ride today, and long day in general – my night to cook, so that always adds a whole new level of exhaustion at the end of the day.

Our ride was largely uphill, 73 miles with 3592 feet of elevation gain. Our route was to the northwest with the big Wind River mountain range off to our left and the Wind River Indian reservation on our right.

The start of the ride was a bit scary, we had a big bank of dark clouds in the southeast looming over us, and the weather report said 25-30% chance of rain, so I dressed warmly and I packed every bit of my rain gear in my backpack as well as my heavy gloves. Oddly though as we rode north the clouds faded and it turned into a really nice day. Certainly not hot, but warm enough that I could use my lighter gloves and hat.
