Day 67: Baker City OR to John Day OR

Jun 25, 2025

Monster ride today – 81 miles, 4700 feet of elevation gain, three big climbs, light headwinds on most of it, 6 hours 40 minutes riding time. On paper and in practice it was very challenging, no question.

It was also a day for some certified grade-A crazy behavior on the part of one driver we encountered early in the day. I was riding with Tim and Mike, we were going slowly to warm up and chatting about something or other, and at a certain point we got passed by what looked like a dark red minivan with a mountain bike strapped on the back. The van driver yelled something, which we couldn’t make out, but we’re so used to these sorts of encounters it barely registered. Usually in these situations the driver is unhappy that they had to slow down to pass the bikes safely, or they just don’t like bikes on the road, or whatever.
