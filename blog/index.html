Another challenging ride today, they seem to be coming thick and fast in this part of the tour. Gone are the 45 and 50 mile days at least for now.

Yellowstone for some reason doesn’t allow cyclists to enter the park at the south entrance (?), so we actually wound up piling all our bikes onto the van then hopping in the van ourselves, at which point Dan drove us into the park to Grant Village.
