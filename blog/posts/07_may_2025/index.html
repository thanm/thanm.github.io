Day 18: Franklin IN to Cloverdale IN

Posted on May 7, 2025

Shorter day today, 55 miles, which I think was a big relief to everyone, we were all still worn out this morning after yesterday’s super-challenging ride. Today’s ride was also mostly on nice back roads, and the winds were well-behaved, so it was a pleasure all around.

I rode with Mel and Chet today, and spent a fair amount of time in the “pack”, not trying to push the pace (much needed after yesterday).

Approaching Martinsville, near the halfqway point of the ride:

Camping in Lieber State Recreation Area near Cloverdale, seems like a pretty basic state park, showers and electricity but not a lot else. The cell coverage is unfortunately really poor, enough for short text messages but little else (blogging and using my laptop via hotspot are completely out of the question, and cell calls seem to get dropped after about 15 or 20 seconds).

Tomorrow is going to be laundry day; Dennis has promised us that there is a laundromat within easy striking distance of our camping spot – I definitely need to wash some stuff at this point.
