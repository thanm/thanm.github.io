Day 22: Alton IL to Augusta MO

Posted on May 11, 2025

Nice ride today, route distance was 53 miles, but at the end of the day I rode a bit over 60. Very flat, not much in the way of hills. Today was also the first day back (from injury) for our rider Theo – we looked great out on the bike, it was good to see him.

We had a couple of long stops, one for coffee in Saint Charles, then another stop in the town of Defiance, where there was a nice (and extremely crowded) brew pub.

Our campground (“Klondike County Park Campground”) is basic but seems fairly quiet – no wifi and only a few spots for electricity but basic showers and a good spot for gathering near the parking lot.

Lovely full moon in the evening; a bunch of us took a short stroll after dinner around the lake here in camp (it was apparently an old sandstone quarry).
