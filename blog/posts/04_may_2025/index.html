Day 15: Mount Sterling OH to Waynesville OH

Posted on May 4, 2025

The weather forecast when we looked at it last night was grim – all of us were steeling ourselves for a “rain all day” ride, but lo and behold in the morning we had cloudy skies but no precipation, huzzah! We had quick breakfast and headed out (with all our raingear packed along just in case).

Short ride, 52 miles, mostly flat, no headwinds, just a tiny bit of rain … it was great, a veritable “walk in the park”.

Dressed for biking in the rain:

Mike doing his best to repair his biking sandals with duct tape:

Surrounding fields incredibly soaked, all of the rivers and creeks overflowing…

I am cooking dinner tonight with Chet, looking forward to that – we have some extra time on our hands, no need to rush.
