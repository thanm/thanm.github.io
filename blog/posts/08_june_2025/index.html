We had an off day here in Grand Teton National Park, very much needed at this point after two really challenging and long cycling days. Knees still really stiff, in fact I can’t completely bend my left on all the way (eek), feels similar to some of the fallout from my toughest hiking days.

I mainly did laundry and relaxed today– Chet + Tom + I walked over to the restaurant next to the visitor’s center and had a nice breakfast, then later on took a short hike around a nearby peninsula that had nice views of the Tetons, then a pizza lunch with ice cream afterwards (huzzah).
