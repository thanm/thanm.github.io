Trusty bike ... or not?

Posted on Apr 18, 2025

Another training ride yesterday; it was a bit cold and windy but I did a good soldi ride (63 miles) to try to prime the pump for my upcoming tour.

At mile 61 or thereabouts, however, I started to hear an odd clunking noise coming from my front derailleur, and when I looked down I saw that one of my front chainrings (the smaller one) had almost completely come off – of the four bolts holding it on, only one was still there. Eeek.

I limped home for the last two miles and made it up to our driveway. Later that afternoon I brought my bike to the shop to see what they could do for it. There was much head-scratching on the part of the bike mechanics – this is apparently a pretty unusual failure, and the bolts in question were very obscure. I had a nervous 15 minutes or so waiting while they scrounged around in the back of the shop, eventually locating some parts that should (in theory) work.

Mechanical failures kind of go with the territory for biking … but hopefully there won’t be too many more of these while I am out on the road (gulp).
