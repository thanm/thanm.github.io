Day 75: Post-trip checkin

Posted on Jul 4, 2025

One final travel post – made it back to Arlington MA after a marathon airplane/airport odyssey; American canceled my flight out of Eugene due to a mechanical failure and I had to get rebooked on United. Didn’t roll into Logan until 1am, but when I got there Amy was there to pick me up, super happy to see her.

I am a bit scruffier and a bit lighter, the post-trip weigh-in had me at 160 pounds on the scale, so it looks like I’ve dropped a bit over ten pounds. The post-tour version of me:

So glad to be home! Seventy five days is a long time to be on the road, and especially long if you are tent camping for most of the time.

It was great to see my pup Ellie as well, I missed her and all the various parts of my doggie-centric existence (the daily walks, the snuggle sessions on the couch, you name it).

I am going to do a final retrospective post on my gear (and such) in the next couple of days, then probably time to put this blog to bed…
