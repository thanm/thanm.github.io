Day 72: Down to seven riders

Posted on Jun 30, 2025

We are down to seven riders as of today; Chet packed up and left the tour, he is flying back home tomorrow morning. Cause was again not health or injury related, but again probably not something I should delve into on the blog.

I’ll miss him, sorry that he won’t be able to round out the tour and do the wheel dip. He was one of my favorite people to ride with, and I enjoyed his sense of humor and wisecracks (delivered daily in his gravely voice). I guess I will have to drink the remainder of the chocolate milk in the cooler now …
