Day 28: Zero mileage day in Atchison KS

Posted on May 17, 2025

Sunny and windy here in Atchison KS, it is going to be a nice day, although it looks like we’ll be blown around quite a bit during the afternoon. With the exception of the bathrooms I like this campground – the wind keeps the bugs away, and the sleeping conditions are good (cool, quiet). Photo:

Today’s agenda included a bike ride into town (3 or 4 miles) and a session at the laundromat for an extended laundry session, a pizza lunch, and a side trip to the Amelia Earhart home museum.

In fact there are lots of markers and statues and such devoted to Amelia Earhart, it’s clear she is a big part of the town. A statue:

Some pics from the museum:

First swim on this tour! This was in the pond not far from our camping area in Atchison. Nice cool water. A bit hard to get in and out on the dock, but worth it. I waited until mid-afternoon for maximum warmth.

Sunset taken from the Atchison camping area in the evening:

All in all a good rest day. Looking forward to dinner; Tom and Simona are planning on doing a “breakfast for dinner” meal (pancakes maybe? we’ll see).
