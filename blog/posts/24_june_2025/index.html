Day 66: Down to eight riders

Posted on Jun 24, 2025

We lost another one of our riders in Baker City – Mel departed the tour. Not for health or injury, but for other reasons I don’t want to dig into just now. He left while we were in Baker City; we still have his bike on top of the van (at some point someone will figure out how to ship it back).

I will miss Mel, his was a presence on the tour was one that I greatly appreciated. We had some memorable conversations, and I always enjoyed riding and cooking with him. Bummed that he won’t be there in person for the final wheel dip next week (seems ridiculous, but it is what it is, apparently). I’ll leave it at that.
