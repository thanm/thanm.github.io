Day 48: Down to nine riders

Posted on Jun 6, 2025

The bad news of the day today was that our teammate Deb decided to leave the tour, I was bummed to see her message announcing that she was pulling the plug. Super strong rider, but she was apparently just not able to sleep well and was getting more and more exhausted as time went on.

Can’t really fault her for making her exit given the sleep issues – if I was only sleeping an hour a night I think that would be a deal breaker for me as well. It is hard enough to do these rides even with good sleep and nutrition, but without good rest I think it would be nearly impossible.

We wished her the best, I hope that she will be able to rest and recharge and make another go at the second part of the tour at some point.
