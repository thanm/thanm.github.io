Day 65: Halfway OR to Baker City OR

Posted on Jun 23, 2025

Finally a nice warm day with no need to carry rain gear! Yay.

It was a not-too-long ride (53 miles) but with plenty of climbing, 3700 feet of elevation gain. Big climb right off the bat coming out of Halfway, but I actually like my climbs early when it is cool, it was enjoyable. Big downhill, a ride in the valley for a while, then we had long section following the Powder river in a steep, narrow, winding canyon. Then another big climb up through the desert and down into the big valley with Baker City in it.

Terrain very dry, desert-like aside from the few bits of green stuff growing near the rivers and creeks. There are occasional farms, but all the fields are heavily irrigated.
