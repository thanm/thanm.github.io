Day 14: Zero Mileage Day in Deer Creek Lake Park, Mount Sterling OH

Posted on May 3, 2025

Zero day in Mount Sterling Ohio. Weather forecast: rain all day, so a mixed bag – on the one hand we did’t have to ride through the miserable weather, but on the other hand we will have rain for our relaxation day. The group opted to get in the van and drive in to Columbus to explore en masse, I think nobody wanted to sit around in a wet tent all day.

We visited the REI store, I bought a few more rain-related items (waterproof socks, we’ll see how that goes, and some medium-sized dry bags).
