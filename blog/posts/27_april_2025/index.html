Day 8: GAP trail ride from West Newton PA to Pittsburgh PA

Posted on Apr 27, 2025

Cold start today, high 30’s, but it was decided that we would have a late (1 hour delayed) start since we only had 35ish miles to ride.

It was an easy ride, cool, a bit of a headwind, but another enjoyable day out in the sunshine (was better than what we had to deal with the previous day). I rode mostly with Mel; we chatted about our kids and places we’d visited.

Lots of nice old iron bridges over the river, including a couple that the trail ran over.

Here’s the place we are staying in Pittsburgh – the Travelers Rest hotel. This place was fantastic, really enjoyed our stay. It is just about custom-made for bikes – there is a bike garage with tools and lots of storage, a really nice common area where your group can gather, and some excellent rooms. It began life as a cast iron pipe factory back in 1863 (I think), then went through several iterations before being bought and refurbished by the current owners (who are very nice people to boot).

I cooked dinner in the evening with my roommate Theo; that was a certain amount of fun but also a bit stressful since the stove in the TR kitchen was a very underpowered – I had put up a big pot of red lentil soup and it took more than 50 minutes for it to come to a boil (instead of the expected 15), so things were rather delayed. No matter, I think everyone enjoyed the grub.

380 or so miles down, we have no ridden a tenth of our way across the USA, huzzah!

Zero day tomorrow, yay! Good opportunities for laundry, rest, naps, and other such things.
