Day 21: Vandalia IL to Alton IL

Posted on May 10, 2025

Today was a final push through Illinois, riding all the way up to the town of Alton (a St Louis suburb) on the banks of the Mississippi. This was about a 75 mile ride, split halfway between SR40/SR140 and a set of smaller roads and bike trails towards the end of the ride. Again we had great riding conditions, sunny, favorable breezes. Today I rode with the Chet, Mel, Tom and Terrence – they were striking a moderate and not blistering pace (yay).

Ride:

The church that we were supposed to stay at in Alton apparently had a conflict, they were putting on an event, so we got booted out into a nearby Super 8 motel – not the Ritz, but nicer than camping (no tent needs to be pitched, etc).
