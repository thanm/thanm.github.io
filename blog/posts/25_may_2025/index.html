Day 36: McCook NE to Imperial NE

Posted on May 25, 2025

Nice low-intensity ride from McCook to Imperial this morning, we had mild tailwinds for most of it, a tiny bit of rain but mostly just misty/foggy stuff, nothing that would soak our clothing.

We are staying in a Methodist church here in Imperial, yay – super happy about not having to pitch my tent (especially given that it is supposed to rain tonight).

Here I am geared up for the ride ready to leave McCook – hope for the best, prepare for the worst as the saying goes:

Paradise NE at mile 28 basically looked like a ghost town for all intents and purposes; we stopped there hoping for some sort of store but found nothing. Then a pickup truck that was rolling slowly down the street stopped, Terry went over to chat the guy up … he got out his phone and made a call, and lo-and-behold, a bearded man in jeans appeared out of nowhere, unlocked the general store, and we got to go inside and have coffee and snacks.

Post office in Wauneta, mile 45:

Nothing open there except the Sinclair gas station, which (ironically) was really hopping.

A shot of the church where we are staying in Imperial:

We are on mountain time now, as well – gained an hour. Tomorrow we will be crossing over into Colorado, huzzah.

Chet and Mike are cooking, we will see what they whip up in the kitchen.
