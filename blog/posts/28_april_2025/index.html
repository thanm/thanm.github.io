Day 8: Zero day in Pittburgh

Posted on Apr 28, 2025

Rest day here in Pittsburgh PA. It is good to not be under the gun to pack up stuff… just roll out of bed and move on to whatever is on your agenda.

I spent the morning helping Theo (our rider from the Netherlands) wrangle with the people at the cell phone store to get a US cell plan, that was quite the electronic adventure. I had done a little research ahead of time to make sure his phone was compatible, but there was still a lot of drama and complication. The person behind the counter needed lots of nudging and arm-twisting (I think what they really wanted was for him to buy a new phone, but that was not going to happen).

I thought about venturing out to visit the Carnegie art museum, but for some reason my left foot was not in good shape (combination of blisters and a stubbed toe), so I opted for staying local.

Tomorrow we kick off a four day run into Ohio and Indiana, and then after that we have an uninterrupted 13 day stretch without a rest day, whee :-).
