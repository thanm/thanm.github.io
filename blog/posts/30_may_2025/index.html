Day 41: Zero Mileage Day in Fort Collins CO

Posted on May 30, 2025

If there is a better town to have a zero day in, I’m not sure I can imagine it … Fort Collins seems to have it all, nice weather, bikeable streets, free buses, good restaurants, good bike shops, list goes on.

The new sleeping pad that I bought yesterday at REI seems to not fit inside my duffle, as it turns out. Oops. I think what I will need to do is bungy-cord it to the outside or something to that effect. They had a limited selection, so I had pick from what they had and deal with the fallout.

Not a whole lot of blog-worthy stuff happening today, mainly just errands, relaxation, and laundry.

I did cycle past the lovely Poudre river on my way to the UPS store, photo below. There is also supposedly a really nice bike trail that runs along it out to the east, but (for obvious reasons) I don’t think anyone in our group is going to be exploring it. We will see much more of the western part this weekend however.

Dinner in the old town at an Irish pub place, we had a couple of beers and I had a plate of fish and chips.
