Day 5: Down to eleven riders

Posted on Apr 24, 2025

Today in Cumberland one of our riders (Michael P) decided to leave the tour – not an issue of health or injury, there were other factors at work, and I heard later on that he was primarily not mentally game for doing the rest of the tour. Michael is a transam-veteran and a very strong rider, but decided he just didn’t think he would enjoy a whole second tour.

It was a bit of a surprise, but I think there is no point doing one of these multi-month excursions if you really don’t think you are going to enjoy it, so the decision makes sense to me.
